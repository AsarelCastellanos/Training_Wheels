Leadership

At SCLA we teach our kids how to become top notch innnovaters. At the University of Southern California, you will be joining a network of the best innovators. You will make connections with tech professionals that can become your mentors and help you become successful in the tech world.

Academics

In SCLA, you will learn to code using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Python. You will learn how to build your own startup tech business. At SCLA you will learn how to learn. You will master your strength to become the best version of yourself. After a few weeks with us you will understand that success is not hard just a lot of work, but you will thrive.

Community

During 12 weeks, you will become part of a brotherhood of leaders, a brotherhood that inspires you to give it your all. Your brothers will help you code, solve problems, and succeed in life. You will be part of leaders who have changed their communities and families. Today we have TXT’rs from Los Angeles to New York, working to change the world.

Technology

Our lil’s become the best developers. Learning high level programming lanaguages such as Python, Ionic, and Javascript. We also encourgae the Lil’s to learn the whole meanstack as well, which include MongoDB, Express,Angular 6, and Node.
